# User Authentication

## Steps

1. Open the app in your web browser.
2. You will see the "Sign in with ZAPT" heading.
3. Click on the "ZAPT" link to learn more about the platform (opens in a new tab).
4. Use the authentication form to sign up or sign in using your email or social providers (Google, Facebook, Apple).
5. After successful authentication, you will be redirected to your dashboard.
